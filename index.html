FATE MEETS

MALDON

Talk about fate! Maldon is the story of two passionate hairstylists who met on a journey to embrace their passion for beauty and self care. Coming through from diverse backgrounds, starting in Malaysia where their careers began… and onto a fated meet-cute at a Japanese salon in Indonesia. They worked together awhile, finding inspiration to follow their hearts and begin a new life together as partners.

THE DREAM

UNFOLDS

Coming back to Malaysia together, Yves and Stephanie decided to advance their career pursuits and become trailblazers bound by a new idea to make a fashionable, extremely personalised statement amongst KL salons. Onto the end of 2021, the dynamic duo made their passion project official and the Maldon idea took off. Maldon opened its doors in June 2022 with a purpose to connect with the needs of their customers. A minimalist space that aspires to create an ambience that is cozy to provide a sense of warmth for every customer that steps in.

A winning smile AND a confident flip in their hair,

we want our customers feeling chic, confident AND carefree for days.
